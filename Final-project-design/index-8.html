KODA GETS ADOPTED

The pandemic affected a lot of people and animals, but through every storm comes a calm...Mary Matthews has added Koda to her family Mary and koda hit it off the moment they interacted with each other. Koda was rescued and had many injuries and traumas, but over time the injuries healed. Mary was shocked by the story of Koda and she wanted to offer Koda the life Koda deserves.
