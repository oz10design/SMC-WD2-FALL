CRISTIANO RONALDO

    • Cristiano Ronaldo dos Santos Aveiro; (born 5 February 1985) is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Premier League club Manchester United and captains the Portugal national team. Often considered the best player in the world and widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, Ronaldo has won five Ballon d'Or awards and four European Golden Shoes, the most by European player. He has won 32 trophies in his career, including seven league titles, five UEFA Champions Leagues, one UEFA European Championship and one UEFA Nations League. Ronaldo holds the records for most goals(134) and assists (42) in the Champions League, most goals in th European Championship(14), and most international goals by a male player(111). He is one of the few recorded players to have made over 1,100 professional career appearances, and has scored over 780 official senior career goals for club and country.

Sports journalists and pundits regularly weigh in the individual merits of both players in an attempt to argue who they believe is the best palyer in modern football or in the history of the game. It has been compared to several sports rivalries, among them the Muhammad Ali - Joe Frazier rivalry in boxing, the Borg - McEnroe rivalry in tennis and the Senna - Prost rivalry from Formula One motor racing. Some commentators choose to analyze the differing physiques and playing styles of the two. Part of the debate revolves around the contrasting personalities of the two players, as Ronaldo is sometimes depicted as an arrogant and theatrical showoff, while Messi is portrayed as shy, humble character.
